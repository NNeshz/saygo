# Módulo 7 — Voz Pasiva Básica

*Presente y Pasado Simple · 8 horas*


**Estructura básica**

- Presente Simple: The homework is checked by the teacher / English is spoken here
- Pasado Simple: The window was broken by a student / The exam was cancelled
- Negativo: The assignment isn't finished / The meeting wasn't recorded
- Interrogativo: Was the homework collected? / Is Spanish taught in this school?

**"By + agente" (opcional en A2)**

- The cake was made by my mother
- Mexico was conquered by Spain (contexto histórico básico)

**Verbos comunes en voz pasiva**

- make → made / build → built / write → written / invent → invented / discover → discovered
- cancel → cancelled / postpone → postponed / approve → approved / reject → rejected

**Diferencia activa vs. pasiva**

- The teacher graded the exam (activa) vs. The exam was graded by the teacher (pasiva)

**Aplicación práctica**

- Escuela: The exam was postponed / My essay was graded / The students were selected
- Noticias: The road was closed / A new school was built in my neighborhood
- Trámites: The application was approved / My ID card was issued last week


## 1. ¿Para qué sirve esto?

La voz pasiva se usa cuando lo importante es **lo que pasa**, no quién lo hace.
Es muy común en noticias, avisos escolares, trámites y contextos formales.
En inglés, la voz pasiva se forma con **is/are** o **was/were** más el participio pasado del verbo.
Dominar esta estructura te permite leer y escribir textos más complejos y profesionales.


## 2. Estructura y reglas

### (a) Presente Simple pasivo — is / are + participio

| Sujeto | Auxiliar | Participio pasado | Complemento |
|--------|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| The report | **is** | written | every week. |
| The emails | **are** | sent | every morning. |
| The app | **is** | updated | automatically. |
| New buses | **are** | built | in Mexico. |

> Regla: singular → **is** / plural → **are** + participio pasado.

### (b) Pasado Simple pasivo — was / were + participio

| Sujeto | Auxiliar | Participio pasado | Complemento |
|--------|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| The school | **was** | built | in 1985. |
| The event | **was** | cancelled | last Friday. |
| The winners | **were** | selected | by the jury. |
| The laws | **were** | approved | in Congress. |

> Regla: singular → **was** / plural → **were** + participio pasado.

### (c) Formas negativas

| Tiempo | Fórmula | Ejemplo |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Presente | Subject + **is/are not** + participle | The test **is not** postponed. |
| Presente | Subject + **isn't/aren't** + participle | The classes **aren't** cancelled. |
| Pasado | Subject + **was/were not** + participle | The project **was not** approved. |
| Pasado | Subject + **wasn't/weren't** + participle | The documents **weren't** rejected. |

### (d) Preguntas

| Tiempo | Fórmula | Ejemplo |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Presente | **Is/Are** + subject + participle? | **Is** the meeting **postponed**? |
| Pasado | **Was/Were** + subject + participle? | **Were** the results **announced**? |
| Pregunta-wh presente | **When is** + subject + participle? | **When is** the report **published**? |
| Pregunta-wh pasado | **When was** + subject + participle? | **When was** the bridge **built**? |

### "By + agent" — quién hizo la acción

Usa **by + agent** cuando es relevante mencionar quién realizó la acción. Es opcional.

- The vaccine was discovered **by** scientists in 1955.
- The novel was written **by** Gabriel García Márquez.
- The law was approved **by** Congress last month.

> Si no importa quién lo hizo, omite el "by + agent":
> _The exam was postponed._ ✓ (no se sabe o no importa quién lo pospuso)

### ⚠️ Errores frecuentes de estructura

| ❌ Incorrecto | ✓ Correcto |
|--------------|-----------|
| The book is write by her. | The book **is written** by her. |
| The car was build in 2010. | The car **was built** in 2010. |
| The results were announced by them yesterday it. | The results were announced yesterday. |
| Is the meeting cancelled? — Yes, it cancelled. | Yes, it **is cancelled**. |
| The emails are sended every day. | The emails **are sent** every day. |


## 3. Vocabulario clave — Verbos pasivos comunes

| Infinitivo | Participio pasado | Pronunciación | Significado |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------|-------------|
| make | **made** | /meɪd/ | hacer |
| build | **built** | /bɪlt/ | construir |
| write | **written** | /ˈrɪt.ən/ | escribir |
| cancel | **cancelled** | /ˈkæn.səld/ | cancelar |
| postpone | **postponed** | /pəʊstˈpəʊnd/ | posponer |
| approve | **approved** | /əˈpruːvd/ | aprobar |
| reject | **rejected** | /rɪˈdʒek.tɪd/ | rechazar |
| select | **selected** | /sɪˈlek.tɪd/ | seleccionar |
| discover | **discovered** | /dɪˈskʌv.əd/ | descubrir |
| invent | **invented** | /ɪnˈven.tɪd/ | inventar |
| send | **sent** | /sent/ | enviar |
| announce | **announced** | /əˈnaʊnst/ | anunciar |

> Truco: los participios terminados en **-ed** se pronuncian /t/, /d/ o /ɪd/ según el sonido final del verbo base. Los irregulares hay que memorizarlos.

## 5. Tabla de errores comunes

| ❌ Error | ✓ Corrección | Explicación |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| The bridge was build last year. | The bridge **was built** last year. | "Build" es irregular; su participio es **built**. |
| The reports are wrote by the manager. | The reports **are written** by the manager. | "Write" es irregular; su participio es **written**. |
| Was the meeting postpone? | Was the meeting **postponed**? | En preguntas también se necesita el participio, no el infinitivo. |
| The letters were sended this morning. | The letters **were sent** this morning. | "Send" es irregular; no se le agrega -ed. |
| It is made of them yesterday. | It **was made** yesterday. | Tiempo pasado requiere **was/were**, no **is/are**. |
| The law is approve by the president. | The law **is approved** by the president. | Falta la terminación del participio (-d). |
| The phone was invented from Alexander Bell. | The phone was invented **by** Alexander Bell. | El agente se introduce con **by**, no "from". |


## 6. Comparación o contraste

Transforma de activa a pasiva y viceversa:

### Activa → Pasiva

| Oración activa | Oración pasiva |
|---------------|----------------|
| The teacher **cancels** the class. | The class **is cancelled** (by the teacher). |
| Engineers **built** the stadium in 2015. | The stadium **was built** in 2015. |
| The committee **selects** the finalists. | The finalists **are selected** (by the committee). |
| A scientist **discovered** penicillin in 1928. | Penicillin **was discovered** in 1928. |

### Pasiva → Activa

| Oración pasiva | Oración activa |
|---------------|----------------|
| The project **was approved** by the director. | The director **approved** the project. |
| The app **is updated** every week. | The team **updates** the app every week. |
| The report **was written** by María. | María **wrote** the report. |
| New phones **are made** in that factory. | That factory **makes** new phones. |

> Clave: en la voz activa, el sujeto **hace** la acción. En la pasiva, el sujeto **recibe** la acción.


## 7. Diálogo modelo

**Situation:** Valeria and Diego are students at UNAM checking the school notice board.


**Valeria:** Hey, Diego. Is the chemistry exam cancelled today?
**Diego:** Yes, it is. A notice was posted this morning on the board.
**Valeria:** When was it announced? I didn't see anything.
**Diego:** It was sent by email last night. Didn't you check?
**Valeria:** No, I didn't. So when is the new date? Was it already selected?
**Diego:** Not yet. We were told it will be postponed to next week.
**Valeria:** And the lab session? Is it cancelled too?
**Diego:** No, it isn't. The lab is still confirmed.
**Valeria:** Good. Was any reason given for the cancellation?
**Diego:** The professor is sick. The decision was made this morning.
**Valeria:** Okay, thanks. I hope the new date is approved soon.
**Diego:** Me too. I'll let you know when it's announced.


**Traducción:**

**Valeria:** Oye, Diego. ¿El examen de química se canceló hoy?
**Diego:** Sí. Esta mañana se publicó un aviso en el tablero.
**Valeria:** ¿Cuándo se anunció? No vi nada.
**Diego:** Se envió por correo anoche. ¿No lo revisaste?
**Valeria:** No. Entonces, ¿cuándo es la nueva fecha? ¿Ya fue seleccionada?
**Diego:** Todavía no. Nos dijeron que se pospondrá para la próxima semana.
**Valeria:** ¿Y la sesión de laboratorio? ¿También está cancelada?
**Diego:** No. El laboratorio sigue confirmado.
**Valeria:** Bien. ¿Se dio alguna razón para la cancelación?
**Diego:** El profesor está enfermo. La decisión se tomó esta mañana.
**Valeria:** Está bien, gracias. Espero que la nueva fecha se apruebe pronto.
**Diego:** Yo también. Te aviso cuando se anuncie.


## 8. Texto modelo

### School Notice — Universidad del Valle de México

**Important Announcement**

All first-semester students are informed that the registration deadline has been extended.
New documents can be submitted until Friday, May 30th.
All applications will be reviewed by the admissions office.
Students whose documents were rejected last week are asked to resubmit them with corrections.
The final list of accepted students will be published on the official website.
Any incomplete forms will not be processed.
The updated schedule was approved by the Academic Committee on Monday.
Students are encouraged to check the portal regularly.
If your application was not received, please contact the administration office immediately.


**Traducción:**

Todos los estudiantes de primer semestre son informados de que el plazo de inscripción ha sido extendido.
Los documentos nuevos pueden entregarse hasta el viernes 30 de mayo.
Todas las solicitudes serán revisadas por la oficina de admisiones.
Se pide a los estudiantes cuyos documentos fueron rechazados la semana pasada que los vuelvan a enviar con correcciones.
La lista final de estudiantes aceptados será publicada en el sitio oficial.
Los formularios incompletos no serán procesados.
El calendario actualizado fue aprobado por el Comité Académico el lunes.
Se anima a los estudiantes a revisar el portal con regularidad.
Si tu solicitud no fue recibida, comunícate con la oficina de administración de inmediato.


## 9. Ejercicios de práctica

### Ejercicio 1 — Completa con la forma pasiva correcta (presente o pasado)

Fill in the blank with the correct passive form of the verb in parentheses.

1. The new stadium __________ (build) in 2018.
2. Spanish __________ (speak) by millions of people around the world.
3. The project __________ (not / approve) by the committee last week.
4. __________ the results __________ (announce) yet?
5. These phones __________ (make) in Mexico.
6. The flight __________ (cancel) due to bad weather yesterday.

### ✅ Respuestas — Ejercicio 1

1. The new stadium **was built** in 2018.
2. Spanish **is spoken** by millions of people around the world.
3. The project **was not approved** by the committee last week.
4. **Have** the results **been announced** yet? / **Were** the results **announced** yet?
5. These phones **are made** in Mexico.
6. The flight **was cancelled** due to bad weather yesterday.


### Ejercicio 2 — Transforma de activa a pasiva

Rewrite each active sentence as a passive sentence.

1. The teacher writes the report every Friday.
2. Scientists discovered a new species last year.
3. The company rejected my application.
4. They select the winners by lottery.
5. Alexander Graham Bell invented the telephone.

### ✅ Respuestas — Ejercicio 2

1. The report **is written** (by the teacher) every Friday.
2. A new species **was discovered** (by scientists) last year.
3. My application **was rejected** (by the company).
4. The winners **are selected** by lottery.
5. The telephone **was invented** by Alexander Graham Bell.


### Ejercicio 3 — Corrige el error en cada oración

Find and correct the mistake in each sentence.

1. The bridge was build five years ago.
2. Are the documents sended by email?
3. The news was announce on television.
4. The books is written in English.
5. The meeting was postpone until Monday.
6. Penicillin was discovered from Fleming.

### ✅ Respuestas — Ejercicio 3

1. The bridge was **built** five years ago.
2. Are the documents **sent** by email?
3. The news was **announced** on television.
4. The books **are** written in English.
5. The meeting was **postponed** until Monday.
6. Penicillin was discovered **by** Fleming.


## 10. Resumen visual

| Tiempo | Afirmativa | Negativa | Pregunta |
|--------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Presente singular | is + participle | is not / isn't + participle | Is + subject + participle? |
| Presente plural | are + participle | are not / aren't + participle | Are + subject + participle? |
| Pasado singular | was + participle | was not / wasn't + participle | Was + subject + participle? |
| Pasado plural | were + participle | were not / weren't + participle | Were + subject + participle? |
| Agente opcional | by + person/thing | — | By whom / By what? |
| Verb: make | → made | ❌ maked | ✓ made |
| Verb: build | → built | ❌ builded | ✓ built |
| Verb: write | → written | ❌ wrote (no es participio) | ✓ written |
| Verb: cancel | → cancelled | ❌ cancelld | ✓ cancelled |
| Verb: send | → sent | ❌ sended | ✓ sent |
