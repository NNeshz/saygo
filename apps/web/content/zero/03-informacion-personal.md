# Información Personal y Preguntas Esenciales

**TO BE Interrogativo + WH-**

*Cerrando TO BE + Posesivos + WH- básicas — 10 a 12 horas*

---

## TO BE — Negativo

| Forma completa | Contracción |
|---------------|-------------|
| I am **not** | I**'m not** |
| You are **not** | You **aren't** |
| He is **not** | He **isn't** |
| She is **not** | She **isn't** |
| It is **not** | It **isn't** |
| We are **not** | We **aren't** |
| They are **not** | They **aren't** |

```
I'm not from Brazil.   (I amn't ❌ — no existe)
He isn't tall.
They aren't students.
```

> **I'm not** es la única forma válida para la negación de *I am*. No existe *I amn't*.

---

## TO BE — Interrogativo (Yes/No)

Para hacer preguntas de sí/no, invertí el sujeto y el verbo:

| Pregunta | Respuesta afirmativa | Respuesta negativa |
|----------|---------------------|--------------------|
| **Am** I...? | Yes, you are. | No, you aren't. |
| **Are** you...? | Yes, I am. | No, I'm not. |
| **Is** he...? | Yes, he is. | No, he isn't. |
| **Is** she...? | Yes, she is. | No, she isn't. |
| **Is** it...? | Yes, it is. | No, it isn't. |
| **Are** we...? | Yes, we are. | No, we aren't. |
| **Are** they...? | Yes, they are. | No, they aren't. |

```
Are you a doctor?   →   Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
Is she from Italy?  →   Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.
```

> En las respuestas cortas **no se usan contracciones en afirmativo**: *Yes, I **am**.* (no *Yes, I'm.*) En negativo sí: *No, I**'m not**.*

## Adjetivos posesivos

Indican a quién pertenece algo. Van antes del sustantivo:

| Pronombre sujeto | Adjetivo posesivo | Ejemplo |
|-----------------|-------------------|---------|
| I | **my** | **My** name is Ana. |
| You | **your** | What is **your** job? |
| He | **his** | **His** car is red. |
| She | **her** | **Her** mother is a teacher. |
| It | **its** | **Its** name is Max. |
| We | **our** | **Our** house is big. |
| They | **their** | **Their** children are young. |

```
My name is Carlos.       →  Me llamo Carlos.
Your book is here.       →  Tu libro está aquí.
His sister is a nurse.   →  Su (de él) hermana es enfermera.
```

---

## WH- questions con TO BE

Estas preguntas piden información específica — no se responden con sí/no:

| WH- | Pregunta | Respuesta típica |
|-----|----------|-----------------|
| **What** | What is your name? | My name is... / I'm... |
| **What** | What is this / that? | It's a book / a pen. |
| **Where** | Where are you from? | I'm from Mexico. |
| **Where** | Where is he? | He's at home / at school. |
| **Who** | Who is she? | She's my sister. |
| **Who** | Who are they? | They're my friends. |
| **How** | How are you? | Fine, thanks. / Not bad. |
| **How old** | How old are you? | I'm 25 (years old). |
| **How old** | How old is he? | He's 30. |
| **Which** | Which color do you prefer? | I prefer blue. |

### Estructura de las WH- questions

```
WH-  +  TO BE  +  Sujeto  +  ?

What   is    your name?
Where  are   you   from?
How    old   is    she?
Who    is    he?
```

### Diálogo modelo

```
A: Where are you from?
B: I'm from Colombia. And you?
A: I'm from Venezuela. How old are you?
B: I'm 22. How old are you?
A: I'm 25. What's your job?
B: I'm a student. And you?
A: I'm a teacher.
```
