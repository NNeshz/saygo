# Rutinas Diarias

**Presente Simple — Afirmativo**

*El segundo pilar gramatical — 10 a 12 horas*

---

## Presente Simple — Afirmativo

Usamos el Presente Simple para hablar de **hábitos**, **rutinas** y **hechos generales**.

### Formación

| Sujeto | Verbo | Notas |
|--------|-------|-------|
| I / You / We / They | verbo base | I **work** / They **eat** |
| He / She / It | verbo + **s/es** | He **works** / She **watches** |

### Reglas de la -s/-es en 3ª persona singular

| Terminación del verbo | Regla | Ejemplo |
|----------------------|-------|---------|
| General | + **s** | work → work**s**, play → play**s** |
| -s, -sh, -ch, -x, -o, -z | + **es** | watch → watch**es**, go → go**es** |
| Consonante + y | **-ies** | study → stud**ies**, fly → fl**ies** |

```
He reads every day.       (read → reads)
She watches TV at night.  (watch → watches)
It goes fast.             (go → goes)
My sister studies English.(study → studies)
```

## Marcadores de tiempo

### Preposiciones esenciales: at / on / in

| Preposición | Uso | Ejemplos |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| **at** | horas exactas | at 7 o'clock, at noon, at night, at midnight |
| **on** | días y fechas | on Monday, on Christmas Day, on my birthday |
| **in** | períodos largos | in the morning, in July, in summer, in 2026 |

```
I wake up at 7 o'clock.
I go to school on Monday.
I study in the morning.
```

### Expresiones de frecuencia con tiempo

```
every day / every morning / every weekend
on weekdays / on weekends
once a week / twice a month
```

## Adverbios de frecuencia

Expresan **con qué frecuencia** ocurre algo:

```
always  >  usually  >  often  >  sometimes  >  never
 100%       75-90%     50%        25-30%        0%
```

### Posición en la oración

- **Antes del verbo principal:** I **always** eat breakfast. / She **usually** reads at night.
- **Después de TO BE:** I am **always** tired. / He is **never** late.

```
I always wake up at 7.          ✓
She is usually at home.         ✓
They sometimes go to the gym.   ✓
He never eats meat.             ✓
```

## Las horas

| Expresión | Español | Ejemplo |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| o'clock | en punto | It's 3 **o'clock**. |
| half past | y media | It's **half past** 4. (4:30) |
| quarter past | y cuarto | It's **quarter past** 8. (8:15) |
| quarter to | menos cuarto | It's **quarter to** 6. (5:45) |

```
What time is it?

It's 7 o'clock.        →  Son las 7 en punto.
It's half past 10.     →  Son las 10 y media.
It's quarter past 3.   →  Son las 3 y cuarto.
It's quarter to 9.     →  Son las 9 menos cuarto.
```

## Rutina modelo

```
I wake up at 7 o'clock every day.
I have breakfast at 7:30.
I go to work on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday.
I usually have lunch at 1 o'clock.
In the evening, I often watch TV or read.
I always go to bed at 11 o'clock.
On weekends, I sometimes go to the gym.
```
