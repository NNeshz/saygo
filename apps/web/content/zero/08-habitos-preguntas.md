# Hábitos y Preguntas

**Presente Simple Negativo/Interrogativo + WH- con DO/DOES**

*Completando el Presente Simple — 12 a 14 horas*

---

## Presente Simple — Negativo

Se forma con **don't / doesn't** + verbo en infinitivo:

| Sujeto | Auxiliar negativo | Verbo |
|--------|------------------|-------|
| I / You / We / They | **don't** (do not) | infinitivo |
| He / She / It | **doesn't** (does not) | infinitivo |

```
I don't eat meat.
You don't work on Sundays.
He doesn't like coffee.
She doesn't go to the gym.
```

> **Regla clave:** Con *doesn't*, el verbo vuelve al infinitivo — **sin -s**: *She doesn't work**s*** ❌ → *She doesn't **work*** ✓. El *-s* ya está en *doesn't*.

---

## Presente Simple — Interrogativo (Yes/No)

Se forma con **Do / Does** + sujeto + verbo en infinitivo:

| Auxiliar | Sujeto | Verbo | ? |
|----------|--------|-------|---|
| **Do** | I / you / we / they | infinitivo | ? |
| **Does** | he / she / it | infinitivo | ? |

| Pregunta | Resp. afirmativa | Resp. negativa |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Do you work? | Yes, I do. | No, I don't. |
| Does he eat fish? | Yes, he does. | No, he doesn't. |
| Do they study? | Yes, they do. | No, they don't. |

---

## WH- Questions con DO / DOES

| WH- | Pregunta | Uso |
|-----|----------|-----|
| **What** | What do you do? | profesión / actividad |
| **Where** | Where does he work? | lugar |
| **When** | When do you get up? | tiempo |
| **Why** | Why do you study English? | razón |
| **How** | How do you go to work? | modo de transporte |
| **How many** | How many brothers do you have? | cantidad (contable) |

### Estructura

```
WH-  +  do/does  +  sujeto  +  verbo base  +  ?

What     do      you      eat      for breakfast?
Where    does    she      work?
When     do      they     study?
Why      does    he       wake up early?
```

### Respuestas típicas

```
What do you do?              →  I'm a teacher. / I'm a student.
Where does she work?         →  She works at a hospital.
How do you go to school?     →  By bus. / On foot. / By car.
Why do you study English?    →  Because I want to travel.
```

## How often — Frecuencia

*How often* pregunta **con qué frecuencia** se hace algo:

```
How often do you exercise?
→  Every day. / Once a week. / Twice a month. / Never.
```

| Respuesta | Significado |
|-----------|-------------|
| Every day | todos los días |
| Once a week | una vez a la semana |
| Twice a week | dos veces a la semana |
| Three times a month | tres veces al mes |
| Rarely | raramente |
| Never | nunca |

## Conectores básicos

| Conector | Función | Ejemplo |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **and** | adición | I like pizza **and** pasta. |
| **but** | contraste | I like pizza **but** I don't like cheese. |
| **because** | causa | I study English **because** I want to travel. |
| **so** | resultado | I was tired **so** I went to bed early. |

```
I wake up early and I have breakfast at 7.
I like coffee but I don't like tea.
I study every day because I want to improve.
She doesn't have a car so she goes by bus.
```

## Diálogo modelo

```
A: What do you do?
B: I'm a nurse. I work at a hospital.
A: Where do you work exactly?
B: At the City Hospital. And you, what do you do?
A: I'm a student. I study English and Business.
B: Why do you study English?
A: Because I want to work in an international company.
B: How often do you have classes?
A: Three times a week. On Monday, Wednesday and Friday.
```
