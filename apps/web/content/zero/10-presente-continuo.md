# Acciones en el Momento Presente

**Presente Continuo**

*El tercer pilar gramatical — 8 a 10 horas*

---

## Formación: am / is / are + verbo -ing

| Sujeto | TO BE | Verbo -ing |
|--------|-------|------------|
| I | **am** | eating |
| He / She / It | **is** | reading |
| You / We / They | **are** | playing |

```
I am eating lunch now.
She is reading a book.
They are playing football.
```

---

## Reglas ortográficas del -ing

| Situación | Regla | Ejemplo |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| General | + **ing** | play → play**ing** |
| Termina en **e** muda | quitar la e + **ing** | make → mak**ing**, write → writ**ing** |
| CVC tónica (consonante-vocal-consonante) | duplicar última consonante + **ing** | run → run**ning**, swim → swim**ming**, sit → sit**ting** |
| Termina en **ie** | ie → **y** + **ing** | lie → ly**ing**, die → dy**ing** |

```
play   →  playing        (general)
make   →  making         (e muda → se quita)
write  →  writing        (e muda → se quita)
run    →  running        (CVC → duplicar n)
swim   →  swimming       (CVC → duplicar m)
sit    →  sitting        (CVC → duplicar t)
lie    →  lying          (ie → y)
```

---

## Formas: afirmativo, negativo, interrogativo

### Afirmativo

```
I am  working.       I'm working.
He is sleeping.      He's sleeping.
They are playing.    They're playing.
```

### Negativo

```
I am not  working.   I'm not working.
He is not sleeping.  He isn't sleeping.
They are not eating. They aren't eating.
```

### Interrogativo

| Pregunta | Resp. afirmativa | Resp. negativa |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Are you working? | Yes, I am. | No, I'm not. |
| Is he sleeping? | Yes, he is. | No, he isn't. |
| Are they playing? | Yes, they are. | No, they aren't. |

```
What are you doing?   →  I'm watching TV.
What is she reading?  →  She's reading a novel.
Are they eating?      →  Yes, they are.
```

## Presente Simple vs. Presente Continuo

Esta es una de las distinciones más importantes del inglés:

| Presente Simple | Presente Continuo |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Hábito / rutina | Acción en este momento |
| Hecho general | Acción temporal |
| *I eat pizza.* | *I'm eating pizza.* |

### Ejemplos comparados

```
I eat breakfast every day.       →  Desayuno todos los días. (hábito)
I'm eating breakfast right now.  →  Estoy desayunando ahora mismo. (ahora)

He works here.                   →  Él trabaja aquí. (su trabajo)
He is working now.               →  Él está trabajando ahora. (acción actual)

She reads a lot.                 →  Ella lee mucho. (hábito)
She is reading a book.           →  Ella está leyendo un libro. (ahora)
```

### Palabras clave

| Presente Simple | Presente Continuo |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| every day / always / usually / never | now / right now / at the moment / look! / listen! |

```
Look! He is running!          →  Presente Continuo (estás viendo la acción)
She always runs in the morning.→  Presente Simple (hábito)
```

## Diálogo modelo

```
A: Hi! What are you doing?
B: I'm studying English. What about you?
A: I'm cooking dinner. Is María there?
B: Yes, she is. She's watching TV in the living room.
A: Is Pedro working today?
B: No, he isn't. He's sleeping — he's tired.
```
