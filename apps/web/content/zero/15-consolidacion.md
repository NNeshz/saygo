### Diálogo

```
A: Excuse me, where is the nearest bank?
B: Go straight on and turn left at the traffic lights.
   It's on the right, opposite the supermarket.
A: Is it far?
B: No, it's about 5 minutes on foot.
```

## Imperativos — Instrucciones

### Afirmativos (verbo base sin sujeto)

```
Open your book.      Listen carefully.
Look at the board.   Sit down, please.
Stand up.            Come in.
Wait here.           Call me.
```

### Negativos (Don't + verbo base)

```
Don't touch that.    Don't worry.
Don't forget.        Don't be late.
Don't smoke here.    Don't run.
```

> Agregar **please** lo hace cortés: *Please sit down.* / *Don't forget, please.*

### Emergencias y supervivencia

```
Help!                  →  ¡Ayuda!
Call the police!       →  ¡Llamá a la policía!
Call a doctor!         →  ¡Llamá a un médico!
Call an ambulance!     →  ¡Llamá a una ambulancia!
How do you spell...?   →  ¿Cómo se escribe...?
```

## Pronombres posesivos

Reemplazan al adjetivo posesivo + sustantivo. Van solos, **sin** sustantivo después:

| Adjetivo posesivo + sustantivo | Pronombre posesivo |
|-------------------------------|-------------------|
| This is **my** book. | This book is **mine**. |
| That is **your** car. | That car is **yours**. |
| It's **his** phone. | The phone is **his**. |
| It's **her** bag. | The bag is **hers**. |
| It's **our** house. | The house is **ours**. |
| They're **their** keys. | The keys are **theirs**. |

```
Is this your umbrella?    →  No, it isn't mine. I think it's hers.
Whose bag is this?        →  It's mine!
That's not ours — it's theirs.
```

> **Distinción clave:**
> - *This is **my** book.* — adjetivo posesivo (antes del sustantivo)
> - *This book is **mine**.* — pronombre posesivo (solo, sin sustantivo)

## Diálogo final integrador

```
A: Excuse me, I'm looking for a clothes shop.
B: There's one on Park Street. Go straight on and turn right.
   It's opposite the cinema.
A: How far is it?
B: About ten minutes on foot.
A: Thanks. Oh, and what's the weather going to be like this afternoon?
B: I think it's going to rain. Take an umbrella.
A: I didn't bring mine. I'll buy one!
B: Good idea. Have a nice day!
A: You too!
```
