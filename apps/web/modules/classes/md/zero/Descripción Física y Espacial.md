| Adjetivo | Significado | Opuesto | Fonética |

### 2.2 Demostrativos (This / That / These / Those)

**La matriz mágica:**

|  | CERCANO (aquí) | LEJANO (allí) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SINGULAR** (1 cosa) | THIS /ðɪs/ | THAT /ðæt/ |
| **PLURAL** (2+ cosas) | THESE /ðiːz/ | THOSE /ðəʊz/ |

**Gestos para recordar:**

- This: Señalas algo tocando tu pecho (cerca de ti)
- That: Señalas lejos con el dedo
- These: Abres las manos cerca de ti
- Those: Abres las manos señalando lejos

**Ejercicio 5:** Elige el demostrativo correcto.

1. _ (esto cerca, 1 libro) is my book. → This
2. _ (eso lejos, 1 casa) house is big. → That
3. _ (estos cerca, 2 lápices) are red pencils. → These
4. _ (aquellos lejos, 3 árboles) trees are tall. → Those

## 🗓️ BLOQUE 4: Preguntando con HOW

### 4.1 HOW + Adjetivo (Preguntas de medida)

**Estructura:**

- How + adjetivo + *be* + sujeto?

**Ejemplos:**

- How tall is he? → ¿Qué tan alto es?
- How old is she? → ¿Qué edad tiene?
- How big is your house? → ¿Qué tan grande es tu casa?

**Respuestas típicas:**

- He is very tall.
- She is 25 years old.
- It is too big.

**Ejercicio 8:** Formula las preguntas para estas respuestas.

1. ___? → He is 1.80 meters. (How tall is he?)
2. ___? → She is 30 years old. (How old is she?)
3. ___? → It is very big. (How big is it?)

---

### 4.2 Muy Importante: VERY vs TOO

| VERY | TOO |
| --- | --- |
| Muy - Intensificador positivo | Demasiado - Exceso NEGATIVO |
| Very tall (muy alto, está bien) | Too tall (demasiado alto, golpea el techo) |
| Very expensive (muy caro, describe) | Too expensive (demasiado caro, NO puedo comprar) |
| Very hot (muy caliente, disfrutable) | Too hot (demasiado calor, me quemo) |

**Clave:** Si usas TOO, hay un problema.

**Ejercicio 9:** Very o Too?

1. This coffee is _ hot! I can't drink it. (too)
2. She is _ nice. I like her. (very)
3. The house is _ small. My family doesn't fit. (too)
4. He is _ tall. He plays basketball well. (very)

## 🗓️ BLOQUE 5: Descripción Completa

### 5.1 La Estructura del Párrafo

**Plantilla para describir a alguien:**

Paso 1: Presentación

> This is my friend. / That is my teacher.
> 

Paso 2: Apariencia física

> He/She is tall/short. He/She is young/old.
> 

Paso 3: Edad

> He/She is _ years old.
> 

Paso 4: Detalles específicos

> His/Her hair is _ (color). His/Her eyes are _.
> 

Paso 5: Opinión

> He/She is very nice/funny. / He/She is too serious.
> 

---

### 5.2 Ejemplo Completo

> This is my brother. He is tall and young. He is 20 years old. His hair is black and his eyes are brown. He is very funny.
> 

**Nota:** Recuerda cambiar His (para él) / Her (para ella) según el género.
