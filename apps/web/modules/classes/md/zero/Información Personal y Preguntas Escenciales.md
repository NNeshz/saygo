- **Afirmativo:** He is happy

## BLOQUE 3: Respuestas Cortas

### ⚡ Regla de Oro

Sin complemento → **no** contraer al final

- ❌ Yes, I'm
- ✅ Yes, I am
- ✅ Yes, I'm happy *(con complemento sí se puede)*

### 📊 Tabla de Respuestas

| Pregunta     | Respuesta corta (+) | Respuesta corta (−) |
| ------------ | ------------------- | ------------------- |
| Am I…?       | Yes, you are        | No, you aren't      |
| Are you…?    | Yes, I am           | No, I'm not         |
| Is he…?      | Yes, he is          | No, he isn't        |
| Is she…?     | Yes, she is         | No, she isn't       |
| Is it…?      | Yes, it is          | No, it isn't        |
| Are we…?     | Yes, we are         | No, we aren't       |
| Are they…?   | Yes, they are       | No, they aren't     |

## BLOQUE 6: Preguntas WH-

### 🗝️ Las Llaves de la Información

| Palabra | Pregunta                         | Respuesta        |
| ------- | -------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| What    | ¿Qué? / ¿Cuál? *(cosa)*          | Nombre, objeto   |
| Where   | ¿Dónde?                          | Lugar            |
| Who     | ¿Quién?                          | Persona          |
| How     | ¿Cómo? *(estado)*                | Fine, good, bad  |
| How old | ¿Cuántos años?                   | Número           |
| Which   | ¿Cuál? *(opción)*                | Esta o esa       |

### 📝 Estructura

`[WH-] + [To Be] + [Sujeto] + [?]`

- What is your name?
- Where are you from?
- How old is he?

### ✏️ Ejercicios

**1. Une con flechas:**

- What is your name? → 1. I am 20 years old
- Where are you from? → 2. I am fine
- How are you? → 3. I am John
- How old are you? → 4. I am from Mexico
- Who is he? → 5. He is my brother
- Which is your bag? → 6. The red one

**2. Formula la pregunta:**

- a) My name is Peter → What is your name?
- b) I am from Spain → _______
- c) She is my sister → _______
- d) I am 25 years old → _______
- e) The blue pen → _______ *(Which…?)*

**3. Responde sobre ti:**

- a) What is your favorite color? → _______
- b) Where is your house? → _______
- c) Who is your best friend? → _______
- d) How old is your mother? → _______

## BLOQUE 7: Integración Total

### 🔄 Las Tres Formas

| Contexto | +                    | −                         | ?                    |
| -------- | -------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Identidad | I am John           | I'm not Mary              | Am I John?           |
| Origen   | I am from Spain      | I'm not from Italy        | Where are you from?  |
| Edad     | I am 25              | I'm not 30                | How old are you?     |
| Posesión | This is my book      | This isn't your pen       | Whose is this?       |
